Coronary Artery Disease 
or Coronary Heart Disease
Coronary artery disease (CAD) is one of the leading 
causes of death. It has many causes, all of which 
result in a reduced blood supply to the vital myo-
cardial tissue.
MYOCARDIAL INFARCTION

less) and undergoes necrosis (pathological tissue death). The
