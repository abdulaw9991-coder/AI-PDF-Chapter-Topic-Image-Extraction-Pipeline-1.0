Ankle Joint
The ankle joint (talocrural articulation) is a hinge-type syno-
vial joint. It is located between the distal ends of the tibia and 
the ﬁ
 bula and the superior part of the talus (Figs. 5.95 and 
5.96). The ankle joint can be felt between the tendons on the 
anterior surface of the ankle as a slight depression, approxi-
mately 1 cm proximal to the tip of the medial malleolus.
