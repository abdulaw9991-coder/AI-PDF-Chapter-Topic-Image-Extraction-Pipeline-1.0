the body. The right side of the heart (right heart) receives 
poorly oxygenated (venous) blood from the body through the 
SVC and IVC and pumps it through the pulmonary trunk and 
arteries to the lungs for oxygenation (Fig. 1.49A). The left 
side of the heart (left heart) receives well-oxygenated (arte-
rial) blood from the lungs through the pulmonary veins and 
pumps it into the aorta for distribution to the body.
