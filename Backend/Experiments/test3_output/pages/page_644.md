The cutaneous innervation of the foot is supplied (Fig. 5.72; 
Table 5.15):
• Medially by the saphenous nerve, which extends distally to 
the head of 1st metatarsal.
• Superiorly (dorsum of foot) by the superﬁ

• Laterally by the sural nerve, including part of the

• Posteriorly (heel) by medial and lateral calcaneal branches
