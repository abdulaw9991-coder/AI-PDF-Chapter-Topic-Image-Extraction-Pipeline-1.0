Scalp
The scalp consists of skin (normally hair bearing) and subcu-
taneous tissue that cover the neurocranium from the superior 
nuchal lines on the occipital bone to the supra-orbital margins 
of the frontal bone (Figs. 7.3 and 7.4A). Laterally, the scalp 
extends over the temporal fascia to the zygomatic arches.
