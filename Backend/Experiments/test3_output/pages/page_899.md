Inside each cavernous sinus is the internal carotid artery 
with its small branches, surrounded by the carotid plexus 
of sympathetic nerve(s), and the abducent nerve (CN VI) 
(Fig. 7.31C). The oculomotor (CN III) and trochlear (CN IV) 
nerves, plus two of the three divisions of the trigeminal nerve 
(CN V) are embedded in the lateral wall of the sinus. The
