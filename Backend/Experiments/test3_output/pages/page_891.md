Sebaceous Cysts
The ducts of sebaceous glands associated with hair 
follicles in the scalp may become obstructed, result-
ing in the retention of secretions and the formation of 
sebaceous cysts (pilar cysts). Because they are in the skin, seba-
ceous cysts move with the scalp.
