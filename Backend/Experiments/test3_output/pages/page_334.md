PARASYMPATHETIC INNERVATION
The parasympathetic part of the autonomic innervation of the 
abdominal viscera (Figs. 2.88 and 2.89) consists of the following:
• Anterior and posterior vagal trunks.
• Pelvic splanchnic nerves.
• Abdominal (para-aortic) autonomic plexuses and their 
extensions, the peri-arterial plexuses.
• Intrinsic (enteric) parasympathetic ganglia.
