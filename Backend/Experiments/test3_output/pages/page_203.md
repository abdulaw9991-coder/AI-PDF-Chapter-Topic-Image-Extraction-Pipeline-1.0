The apex beat is the impulse that results from the apex 
of the heart being forced against the anterior thoracic wall 
when the left ventricle contracts. The location of the apex 
beat (mitral area, M) varies in position and may be located 
in the 4th or 5th intercostal spaces, 6–10 cm from the AML 
(anterior median line).
