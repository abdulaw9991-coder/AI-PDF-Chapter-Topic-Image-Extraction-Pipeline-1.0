HAND
The hand is the manual part of the upper limb distal to the 
forearm. The wrist is located at the junction of the forearm 
and hand. Once positioned at the desired height and location 
relative to the body by movements at the shoulder and elbow, 
and the direction of action is established by pronation and 
supination of the forearm, the working position or attitude 
(tilt) of the hand is adjusted by movement at the wrist joint.
