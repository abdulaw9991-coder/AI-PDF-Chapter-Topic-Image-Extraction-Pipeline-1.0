INTRA-ARTICULAR LIGAMENTS OF KNEE JOINT
The intra-articular ligaments within the knee joint consist of 
the cruciate ligaments and menisci. The tendon of the poplit-
eus is also intra-articular during part of its course.

the joint capsule of the joint but outside the synovial cavity
