Fascia of Posterior Abdominal Wall
The posterior abdominal wall is covered with a continuous 
layer of endoabdominal fascia that lies between the pari-
etal peritoneum and the muscles (Fig. 2.95B). The fascia 
lining the posterior abdominal wall is continuous with the 
transversalis fascia that lines the transversus abdominis
