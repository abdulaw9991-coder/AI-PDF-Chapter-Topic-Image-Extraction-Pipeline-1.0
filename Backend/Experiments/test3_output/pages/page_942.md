Corneal Ulcers and Transplants
Damage to the sensory innervation of the cornea from 
CN V1 leaves the cornea vulnerable to injury by for-
eign particles. People with corneal lesions (scarred or 
opaque corneas) may receive corneal transplants from donors 
or implants of non-reactive plastic material.
