To summarize systemically, the order of the major structures 
in the superior mediastinum, from anterior to posterior, is: 
(1) thymus, (2) veins, (3) arteries, (4) airway, (5) alimentary 
tract, and (6) lymphatic trunks.

internal jugular and subclavian veins. At the level of the infe-
rior border of the 1st right costal cartilage, the brachioce-
